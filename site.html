Sterilization

We work only with sterile tools.

We go through all the stages of sterilization

The entire process of decontamination of manicure and pedicure tools is divided into 4 stages. #1 Disinfection. - Keeping the instrument in the disinfection solution, rinsing the instrument under running water from the remains of the disinfection solution. #3 Sterilization. - is made by a high-temperature method, i.e. using dry heat. #4 Storage. - storage is carried out in kraft bags.
